A Short Study on Butterflies

Butterflies are absolutely adorable little insects. They make up 10% of the Lepidoptera order, with a total of 18,000 species globally today.

This is a Peacock Butterfly. How beautiful are its vibrant peacock wings!

  • Scientific Name: Aglais io
  • Family: Nymphalidae
  • Lifespan: 11 months
  • Found in: Temporal regions of Europe and Asia

This is the Karner Blue. It is a subspecies of Melissa Blue!

  • Scientific Name: Lycaeides melissa samuelis
  • Family: Lycaenidae
  • Lifespan: 5 - 14 days
  • Found in: Northeast United States

A Scarlet Mormon Butterfly, named after Chancellor Nikolay Rumyantsev.

  • Scientific Name: Papilio rumanzovia
  • Family: Papilionidae
  • Lifespan: 14 days
  • Found in: Southeastern Asia
